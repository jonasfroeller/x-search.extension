Features

Everything X search
should have been.

Indexed locally. Instantly searchable. Zero data leaves your browser.

Full-Text Search

Search across every indexed profile at once. Exact and partial matches, ranked by relevance.

Per-Profile Search

Drill into a single profile. View indexed count, last sync time, and sync status at a glance.

Result Highlighting

Matches are highlighted inline. See exactly what you searched for in context.

Pause & Resume

Large profiles take time. Pause indexing and pick up exactly where you left off later.

100% Local

Posts live in IndexedDB. No server, no tracking, no accounts. Complete privacy by design.

Virtual DOM Aware

Handles X's lazy-loaded timeline and virtual DOM. Captures posts as the page scrolls through them.

How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No APIs, no accounts, no configuration.

01

Visit a profile

Go to any X profile page. The extension detects it automatically and shows controls in the popup.

02

Click Index

Hit the button. The extension scrolls through the entire timeline, capturing every post into your local database.

03

Search instantly

Type a query. Results appear instantly with highlighted matches across all indexed profiles.
